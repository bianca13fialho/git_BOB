OMFI: Fighting for a better tomorrow!

OMFI (Or, Organization for Mantaining the Future Incident) is an international funded organization created after the Happening, focused on fighting any Nightmare threat and mantaining a safe future for humanity. The division leaders of OMFI were past staff of the Ultimate Academy for the Prodigy Youth.

As the circumstances behind the creation of this organization were special and classified, OMFI is allowed to act independently and outside of international law. To safely mantain the current position of the world, OMFI has complete authority in any circumstances when Nightmare threat is suspected.
