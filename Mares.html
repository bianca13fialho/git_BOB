The Nightmares first appeared after the Happening, the destruction of Greendale made it clear that something was happening around the Ultimate Project and Atlas Project, they were silent and hid in the shadows at first, but eventually started making themselves known to the world. Now, the Nightmares are causing more destruction around the globe, and have finally revealed themsevles to the general public by the creation of the Killing Games.

The Nightmares have a special hierarchy amongst themselves and their followers, it follows: - The Leader (1 known individual) - The general (1 known individual) - Terrors (13 known individuals) - Followers (100 known individuals) - Dreams (unknown) - Meat (unknown)
