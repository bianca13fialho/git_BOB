About:

Lewis Hood is the current Head of Central Intelligence, rising to this post from an intelligence asset. Lewis was hired to OMFI at 02/03/19, being personally recommended by Grey Müller (DECEASED), the previous leader of the Chemical Science Department. Lewis had previous connections to the Ultimate Academy for the Prodigy Youth, being invited by the head teacher Helena Winters (U-Teacher) to serve as a technician. Lewis worked at the academy for eight months along the other employees, with the help of Jonas Munoz (U-Neurosurgeon) and Mariah Schmid (U-Psychologist), Lewis managed to develop the Tri-Neuron Scanner, a machine capable of mapping the entire network of neurons in the human brain. That technology was lost and presumably stolen by the Nightmares. Lewis is among the other staff who disappeared at the night which concluded the Happening.
