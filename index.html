Some intel:

At 02/07/20, 20:02 PM, an event shook the world, all television and radio airwaves were hijacked by unknown individuals and some sort of distorted reality show started being broadcast. This show entitled "Objective: Fear" had 20 young kids trapped on an island resort inside a dome, the host, a fox plushie named Vulpe, explained the rules of the game and that the only way to win and escape was to kill someone and get away with it. This kickstarted a global event "The World's Nightmare".

The show is live 24/7 on every channel and some radio frequencies. All attempts of ending the broadcast have proven themselves ineffective, OMFI's last resort is to forcefully remove the kids from the island.

The rules of the game are:
#1: Students are required to live at the island for the remainder of the unforeseeable future.
#2: When a murder is committed in the island, a Class Trial will be conducted. Participation in this trial is mandatory for all surviving students.
#3: A maximum of two murders can occur during each turn of the killing game.
#4: If the killer is correctly identified during the Class Trial, only the culprit will be punished for their crime.
#5: If the culprit cannot be identified, or if an incorrect student is identified as the culprit, all students except the culprit will be punished for the crime.
#6: If the culprit survives the Class Trial, they are declared the winner. At which point, they will get to leave the island.
#7: "Nighttime" is officially designated as the hours between 10:00 p.m. and 8:00 a.m. During this time, the dining hall and kitchen are closed.
#8: All acts of violence toward Vulpe, the headmaster, are strictly prohibited. In addition, grabbing or moving the Vulpe plushies is considered violence.
#9: Do not destroy island property, that includes cameras, screens, windows, doors and robots.
#10: Vulpe will never directly participate in a murder.
#11: Your Vulpads are very important items. Make sure you do not damage them.
#12: Students who violate these rules will be killed by the giant spider or one of the many drones (they have cameras and guns).
#13: Any more rules can be added if needed.
