Daily notes of events

02/07/20

The day when the games began. It started off as a normal boring day, not much was happening on the news. But suddenly at 20:02 PM all television devices displayed what appeared to be emergency broadcasts or no signal messages.

After 20 minutes of confusion everything was cleared out as devices started showing an image of a fox in a fancy room, the fox called itself "Vulpe" and started acting as a host of a reality show, presenting the story, settings and location.

As the host spoke, people started freaking out and stormed to social media, all trending tabs on Twitter were occupied by this event. It soon became obvious that someone had hijacked the airwaves, but nobody knew the extent of it.

After presenting everything, there was a pause, enough time for people to realize how far this was. Reports of the event were coming from the US, Canada, Mexico, Brazil, England, France, Germany, Russia, China, Egypt, the whole world was watching the exact same thing.

Police stations were flooded with reports all over the world, everyone was going crazy, the world stopped to turn on their televisions and watch. Most people believed that it was a random terrorist attack and that it would be solved quickly, but it wasn't.

Vulpe returned to the screen and started introducing the world to the cast, now known as "Pawns". An image showed pixelarts for every participant of the games. A video started playing, it showed every participant going over to a camera and talking about their lives.

It was after everyone was introduced and Vulpe finished talking that the games began, it started by showing every participant in a garden in the island. They were talking to Vulpe and the bunny, Bure. The rules were explained and Bure was killed as an example. That's when it started.


03/07/20

The first days were chaotic, riots broke out, there were protests, people hoarding resources and hiding in their bunkers all over the world. As some people took this attack as the beggining of the apocalypse, others continued with their lives just avoiding televisions. Nobody was turning on their TV's, nobody wanted to participate in this event. But the people that kept watching realized that whatever this was, it looked a lot like Big Brother.

Law enforcement and military were overwhelmed by calls and people who wanted the trasmission to stop, but it didn't matter what they did, nothing worked. At 12:38 OMFI member [REDACTED] was taken to the CBS Broadcast Center in New York to attempt an interruption of the broadcast. Like other broadcast studios world-wide, this one had been live transmitting the killing games for the majority of the USA East coast.

Agent [REDACTED] tried to turn off the broadcast in all available methods. After two (2) hours, he managed to disrupt the broadcast. Unfortunately, this attempt only lasted for 1:25 minutes before resuming into the live killing games. Other agents reported that in that time, six (6) channels were interrupted.

It was at that moment OMFI administration decided to alter the priority of actions, at first it was stopping the global broadcast, but when that revealed itself to be harder than expected, they decided to locate and stop the games from happening. But first, they had to locate the island. Meanwhile, the person, or people, behind the killing games had to rush their plans.

And at 10:45 AM, the Pawns were summoned to the lobby of the Nightmare Hotel, where they were given their first of many motives. Videos of their deepest darkest secrets were shared at random. Though reluctant, they knew this could be enough to cause some discord.

Meanwhile, OMFI agents were rushing to locate the island to try and stop the killing games. And I should apologize for this, but I need to break the third person narration of events... Two days prior I had noticed that the administration of OMFI seemed nervous and guilty, much like Houston's own security task force and some engineers, it was revealed to us at 18:19 PM that the admins already knew the location of the island, and that secret mission was taking place two months ago, they were the ones who developed the dome.

05/07/20

Some OMFI troops were moved to a secondary island next to Coral Island, Outpost-13 was set up right next to the dome and observation stations were placed in all sides of the dome. The dome occupied an area of 500 feet radius, covering the entire Coral Island, the Outpost-13 island and third smaller island inside.

Blueprints for the giant dome were disclosed, they had been created by [REDACTED], Unfortunately, his body was found shortly after the ending of The Happening.

It was only at 09:53 AM that we were notified of what had occured inside the dome. [REDACTED]'s body was found, she was stabbed overnight in her bedroom. The news came as a shock to everyone, not just in Outpost-13, but all over the world. Her name isntantly started tranding on Twitter, it was all everyone talked about. Suddenly, televisions were being turned on, people were finally interested in the killing games.

Unfortunately, that wouldn't be the only casualty of the day. At 11:02 AM, as Vulpe was explaining what would happen now that the killing games had started, [REDACTED] broke one of the most important rules, attacking Vulpe. This prompted Vulpe to activate his defense robot which shot several darts at [REDACTED]. He would've died if it wasn't for [REDACTED] pushing him out of the way, taking all the darts and dying in his place.

After this series of unfortunate events, the Pawns were ordered to investigate the crime scene and the investigation section of the games began. They would come to an end at 16:16 PM, when Vulpe called everyone to the beach of the island, they got on a yacht which drove itself to the "Trial Island", not before Bure returned from her death in a pink drone. That island was divided in two, one half housed the Trial Room, a small circular structure with a glass ceiling, and the other half housed Outpost-13.

As the Pawns stepped into the Trial Room, the drone-mounted camera entered through a window, finally showing the outside world what it looked like, a circular room with twenty (20) podiums organized in a circle, two (2) of them had grey photos of the deceased Pawns. The Pawns went to their respective podiums, Vulpe and Bure's plushies were placed in a scale mounted on a sword, a demented symbol of justice.

The trial started and the broadcast changed, cameras weren't stable anymore, they were dinamic, flying around the room, spinning and rotating. Bits of information were edited into the footage and they were even doing flashbacks with old recordings, whoever was editing the footage, they were doing a great job at it. The trial lasted for three (3) hours before the Panws reached a verdict, [REDACTED] was found guilty of the murder. And as Vulpe announced that the verdict was correct, the broadcast showed everything that happened during that night.

After giving his final words, [REDACTED] was dragged out of the room into a separate space where he was executed live for the world to see. That event made everyone realize the truth, this wasn't just a prank or a hoax, this was real, someone hijacked the airwaves to display killing games, and it all went downhill after this.
